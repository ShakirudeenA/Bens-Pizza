Menu Screenshot Instructions

  1. The clean menu design is displayed below in the dashed border.
  2. Take a screenshot of just the menu (not including the dashed border).
  3. Save the screenshot as "Clean_Menu.png" in your project folder.
  4. Replace the current menu image in your website with this new image.

Tip: On Windows, you can use the Snipping Tool or press Windows+Shift+S to take a screenshot of a selected area.
